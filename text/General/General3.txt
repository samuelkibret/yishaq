<strong>Name of Element:</strong> Casa Ex-crico ground arch columns<br>
<strong>Reference number:</strong> E-3A-E-C-G-1<br>
<strong>Building:</strong> Casa Ex-crico (E-3A)<br>
<strong>Location:</strong> Ground level<br>
<strong>Function:</strong> Structural<br>
<strong>Heritage Category:</strong> UNESCO identified<br>
<strong>Description:</strong> Casa Ex-crico ground arch columns feature two columns with capital details supporting an arch resembling an oriental arch, with local details at the base of the arch and additional decorative motifs.<br>
<strong>Date:</strong> Expected to have been built during the period of Italian occupation in the 1920s, after the 1921 earthquake.<br>
<strong>Significance:</strong> Arch columns are characteristic features of the architecture of the port city of Massawa.<br>
